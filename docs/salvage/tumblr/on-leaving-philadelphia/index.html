On Leaving Philadelphia

a valediction

Originally posted August 28th, 2012

Words written upon embarking northward, from Philadelphia to Burlington, Vermont. How can one remain in touch with a past community while also opening oneself to a newer one?

So once again I’m faced with the urge to change everything, and I’m still scared of it. But now I feel like that fear is a sign I’m doing the right thing.

There are so many things it greatly pains me to leave behind, but these things are part of the bedrock of what makes me who I am, and mere distance is not enough to remove them from my life. I do not leave because my connection to these things is weak, but because it is strong. So do not fear that I am disappearing forever out of the known world. This is my next adventure—hope to see you in it.

Salvage from Path of Least Efficiency
