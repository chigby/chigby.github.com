The Tenuous Art of Time Travel


In comics, art is king. The authors of the up-and-coming comic book The Tenuous Art of Time Travel needed a website to showcase their art, explain the story and characters, and draw the reader into the moody, exotic world of their creation. This customized Wordpress theme allows for easy deployment and maintenance of galleries, and an expressive canvas on which to illustrate their artwork

Collaboration with illustrator Marcelo Gallegos.
