Quiz of Monster Quizzes

There are many terrible online quizzes in this world. Perhaps you have taken some of them. But perhaps you wonder if there might be something worse out there, something sinister and monstrous lurking under the stairs that other quizzes blithely ascend — and if you wonder this, perhaps you also wonder which one you would be!

Take this quiz to find out which monstrous quiz you are!

1. Which gemstone do you imagine will be placed at the center of your diadem at your coronation?

2. Who shall be the last to remember you?

3. What scent will waft from your sarcophagus at midnight on the anniversary of your death?
