Cameron Higby-Naquin is a programmer, dancer, bibliophile, and star-gazer living in Burlington, VT.

He has performed traditional folkdances of England and Scotland for the Philadelphia Mummers Parade, the New England Folk Festival, the Fair Hill Scottish Games, and the Jane Austen Society of North America’s annual general meeting.

He has written a computer program to classify the age of stars based on their spectroscopic characteristics, and he has written one to look up Magic cards on the command-line.

He reads poetry at The Spoken Rune.

He maintains an abiding fondness for the Kingsessing Morris Men, Germantown Country Dancers, and the Delaware Valley Branch of the Royal Scottish Country Dance Society.
